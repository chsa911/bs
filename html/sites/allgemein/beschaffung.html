How to get pageturners? part one where to buy

There are two areas to this question: Where to buy and how to decide what to buy. The 1. area is easily answered. There are bookshops in many cities and also online. I think the best way to find pageturners is in bookshops with a big choice and the possibility of advice by qualified sales personnel and trial. I recommend here in munich where I live the Hugendubel book shop where I spent many hours reading so I could decide which books to buy.

But bookshops are also in airports and train stations. Here you also usually can try and find a great book. In online bookshops you can also trial read and read recensions: It takes more time though so I recommend the local bookshop, except you want to buy used. But there are shops in some cities also.

I usually buy at Antiquariat Angelika Ziegler Landsberger Str. 71 80339 Munich. Big choice and nice personnel. But for sure there are others If you should be more engaged you might like me visit peddlars markets which can be an interesting experience and a good source for new books and inspiration. Here I find the base for my trial and error strategy.
