bookswiping, a gamechanger

    Swiping on a smartphone means to change content if you have seen enough or don't like it. With paperbooks that seemed impossible. Till I had the idea to cut my books in small pieces. A new world opened up for me.

    This technique gave me a lot of advantages:

    1. advantage: The pages were always close to me when an occasion of reading came up e.g. in the supermarket line or other places

    2. advantage: I could carry many parts of books with me and I could change to another book if I didn't

    like it. Pages I had read I junked asap so I always knew where I stopped the last time.

    I call this the3 R method enabled me an unique reading experience.
