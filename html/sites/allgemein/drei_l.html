The 3-R method or "let things go to gain great pleasure"

1.R: Rip pages into small bits like 25 pages from a book
    and store them in your trousers or jackets pockets.
    This principle is well known from the pocket book concept which
    offered small books for the pocket to have them always with you.
As described in the bookswiping chapter this lets you have your book
    always close to you and easily accessible for the

        brain says. This is easy and fast while the whole
        book with many pages appeared big and tedious. So it says Lets go for
it. Its like fast food. Easy and fast, but of course better for your health.
