My approach is to use every free minute to read.

In most people's imagination reading is best done in quiet and relaxing places like on a couch at a chimney or on the beach or in the train. Even though this is of course a very good thing to do it limits the time to read. With my method I enlarge this timeperiod to any place that doesn 't require my 100% attention. The supermarket line, waiting here is normally enerving. It is the same with waiting in the office for a meeting or during a boring one. Even these meetings might be used. Or think of the time when the computer is busy with downloads or updates. On the way to work or travel. Waiting for the train. Airport checkin and security. Each of these minutes is normally wasted and it can be used for reading. Your book is in the pocket close to you. And you don't have to look for the page you read last. Waiting is reading. Enrich your life with a unique reading experience.
