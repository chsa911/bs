My approach is to use every free minute to read 
In the most people's reading imagination it is best done in quiet and
                relaxing places like on a couch at a chimney or
                on the beach or in the train. Even though this is of course
                a very good thing to do
                it limits the time to read.
                With my method I enlarge this timeperiod to any place that
                doesn 't require your 100% attention. Read in the supermarket line
                that normally is enerving.  In the office waiting for
                a meeting. Even boring meetings might be used or time when the computer is busy
                with downloads or updates. On the way to work or travel. Waiting for the train.
                Airport checkin and security. Every of those minutes normally wasted can be used
                for reading. Waiting is reading. Enrich your life with a unique reading experience.
