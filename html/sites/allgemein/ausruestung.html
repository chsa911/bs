My equipment

There is not a lot I need to apply my reading method...

markers

If the cover is removed you might not remember what book you are reading, especially when you read many at the same time:

That's why I mark the side of the book with my barcode system. I use markers with different colours. This way i can always check with my database which book i am reading.

ducktape

To secure loose pages i use a professional ducktape spender. It's invaluable to keep all pages together.

bookshelf

The bookshelf and
my dreambed

see below are the central part of my reading strategy.

dreambed

Due to a malfunction of my bed. The slatted frame of my bed (parts of it) always fell down.

To prevent this I now store books under my bed and and so I built my dreambed.

google sheets

To administer my reading journey and my barcodes I use google sheets

whatsapp

This messenger helps me to track my reading progress while on the road.

At home I transfer the infos to google sheets.

8. bag

When travelling i take many pages with me in my laptop or shopping bag, so i don't run the risk to run out of good books.

Trashbag or wastepaperbin

When at home or travelling I use a trash bag to get rid of read pages or book debris. The same equipment i use for shopping on my favorite book shopping places
