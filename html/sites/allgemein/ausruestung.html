My equipment

There is not a lot I need to apply my reading method...

markers

If the cover is removed you might not remember what book you are reading especially when you read many at the same time: That's why I mark the side of the book with my barcode system. I use markers with different colours. This way i can always check with my database which book i am reading.

ducktape

To secure loose pages i use a professional ducktape spender it pays to keep all pages together.

cover storage

bookcovers are an emotional bridge to the books I have read. That's why I I keep them. I cut them with a scissor to keep only the minimum and store them in a drawer.

bookshelf

The bookshelf and
my dreambed

see below are the central part of my reading strategy.

dreambed

Due to a malfunction of my bed. The slatted frame of my bed (parts of it) always fell down. I discovered the possibility of books to prevent this and so I can store many books under my bed and so build my dreambed.

google sheets

To administer all the books i am reading and have been reading I use google sheets

whatsapp

To keep information about books when i am traveling I use whatsapp as it is easier to use than google sheets online. At home I transfer the infos to google sheets

8. bag

When travelling i take many pages with me in my laptop or shopping bag, so i don't run the risk to run out of good books. At home I store the pages I want to read in shopping bags for my books always be accessible.

Trashbag or wastepaperbin

When at home or travelling I use a trash bag to get rid of read pages or book debris. The same equipment i use for shopping on my favorite places
