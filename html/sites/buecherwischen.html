bookswiping, the gamechanger

Swiping on a smartphone means to change content if you have seen enough or don't like it. With paperbooks that
    seems impossible. I enable it by cutting my books in small pieces 25 pages for example.
    This gives me a lot of advantages:
1. advantage: I have the pages always close to me when an occasion of reading comes up e.g. in the supermarket line

                
2. advantage: I can carry many parts of books with
    me and I can change to another book if I don't like it
 Pages I have read I junk asap so I always know where I stopped the last time. I call this the 3 R method to read.
 This is the bookswiping principle which creates a unique reading experience.
