The equipment I am using

I don't need many things to apply my reading method...

1. markers

If cover pages are removed it is important to know which book you reading: That's why I mark the side of the book with my barcode system. I use markers with different colours. So even in a big heap I find the book I am looking for. 2. big ducktape

If you tear the books often pages get lost, to prevent this a big ducktape is recommended.

3. cover storage

bookcovers are an emotional bridge to the books I have read so I keep them. I store in transparent plastic bags in a drawer to have access to them all the time.

4. bookshelf

The bookshelf and my dreambed see below are the central part of my reading strategy.

4. dreambed

Due to a malfunction of my bed. The slatted frame of my bed (parts of it) always fell down. I discovered the possibility of books to prevent this and so I can store many books under my bed and so build my dreambed.

5. google sheets

To administer all the books i am reading and have been reading I use google sheets

6. whatsapp

To keep information about books when i am traveling I use whatsapp as it is easier to use than google sheets online. At home I transfer the infos to google sheets

7. bag

When travelling i take many pages with me in my laptop bag so i don't run the risk to run out of good books

8. Trashbag or wastepaperbin

When at home I use a trash bag to get rid of read pages or book debris when travelling I use trash cans. Back to main page
